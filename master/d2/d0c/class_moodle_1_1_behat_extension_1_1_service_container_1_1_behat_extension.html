Moodle\BehatExtension\ServiceContainer\BehatExtension::initialize ( ExtensionManager  $extensionmanager)

Initializes other extensions.

This method is called immediately after all extensions are activated but before any extension configure() method is called. This allows extensions to hook into the configuration of other extensions providing such an extension point.
